How to get started

We conduct a 2-4 week discovery process where we audit your data, interview your stakeholders, and then deliver a recommended set of OKRs and a detailed project plan for 3 months out or more.

Once an SOW has been signed, we will release our kickoff documents and start scheduling workshops ASAP. The goal is to get from signing to planning to operational and driving an impact as quickly as possible.
